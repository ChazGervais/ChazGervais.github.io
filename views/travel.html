Travel

I travel because I am compelled by it. I want to meet locals in other countries and learn more about their cultures. More importantly, I want to work hard with those locals to make improvements to their own livelihoods in a larger mission to form a global community.

I also travel to be inspired by mother nature and all she has to offer. There is abundant natural wonder to be seen. With each stay in a new environment, I am reminded of the forces that bind us to nature in an eternal dance.

I also like to demonstrate how travel can be accomplished without harming nature. By living with locals, taking trains and buses, or cycling across long distances, I hope to see much of the world while showing respect for the planet.
